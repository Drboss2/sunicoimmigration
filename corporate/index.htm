Sunico Immigration Provide an exemplary immigration service for businesses and education providers

		
At Sunico Immigration we have a dedicated team of advisors who guide corporate clients through the complexities of employing workers from outside the UK and Ireland in accordance with the latest UK immigration laws and sponsor compliance. Our clients range from large FTSE organisations through small and medium-sized businesses to brand new start-ups.


Many UK businesses need to attract talent from outside of the UK and it is our job to ensure that the entire process is as smooth and stress free as possible. Our experts can help your business obtain a sponsor licence and look after prospective employees who require appropriate visas and work authorisation.


We also work with schools and universities to ensure they remain compliant within the Student immigration rules, and manage their student visa applications to ensure they do not broach the 10% attrition rates that can result in their licence being suspended or revoked.


It is vital that the correct documentation is in place and visa applications successful, otherwise compliance issues for the company may arise. Therefore, it really is essential that you appoint a specialist such as Sunico Immigration to help you with this process.

Sunico Immigration NO WIN NO FEE PROMISE


We provide a ‘No Win – No Fee’ guarantee for all points-based system visa applications unless expressly stated at the time of appointment. We will guarantee our service for these applications by offering a full refund on our fee should it be unsuccessful.


These guaranteed terms are conditional upon the client being able to demonstrate to the satisfaction of the Home Office that they have earned the income claimed or that they have the necessary funding in place for maintenance or are fully conversant with their business plan in the case of Tier 1 Entrepreneurs.


It also presumes that neither the applicant nor their dependants have previously come under scrutiny or been under investigation by the Home Office for any immigration matter. In order that we can do our job properly the necessary information and details required should be made available and they must genuine as well as accurate.
